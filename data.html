EXPLORE OPPORTUNITY

FOR PLAYERS

  • Exclusive access to U-Hoops and affiliated camps and tours in the U.S. and abroad.
  • Online and telephone advising sessions with sports professionals ranging from athletes to former scouts and coaches.
  • Get recognition and grow your network from your personal profile.

FOR PROFESSIONALS

  • Monthly recommendations of motivated players that fit your team-specific needs in terms of position and skills.
  • Insights into player personalities and tendencies so it is easier to understand what role they will play in a team.
  • Opportunities to promote and gain access for your players to compete in tours and camps in the U.S. and other countries.
